Foreign Student Club

Foreign club is an organization that fosters diversity at Sinhgad Institutes.It is a common platform for all international as well as local students to build a bond of friendship by sharing culture as well knowledge.

Club Aims to

  • Help Exchange Students get acquainted with local culture and traditions
  • Help local students to understand exchange students, their culture

Membership to the club is open to International as well as LOCAL students. Everyone is Welcome to the FAMILY!

Events Planned for Academic Year 2013-14:

  • Forth Nightly Meetings
  • Tutor Programme
  • Country Week
  • Language Courses
  • Trips, Excursions
  • Sports & Cultural Events

Club Structure:

  • President
  • Vice Presidents
  • Secretary
  • Treasurer
  • Webmaster
