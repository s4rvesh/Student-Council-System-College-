Alumni Club

Objectives Of The Club And Modus and Operandi

  • To make an extensive database of the alumni.
  • To associate with the existing departmental/college alumni coordinators.
  • By collecting data from all the existing departmental/college alumni coordinators.
  • Finding common areas of operation between disciplines.
  • To involve alumni in the form of an advisory panel for students.
  • To invite alumni and seek their support for the following activities.

Facilitating Students

  • Industrial/factory visits.
  • Vacation training programs / Internships.
  • Industry supported projects.
  • Mentoring for UG and PG projects.
  • Lectures/seminars/workshops.
  • Placements/career guidance.
  • Promote Research.
