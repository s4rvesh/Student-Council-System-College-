Social Media & PR Club


Objectives:

The club would provide students opportunity to

  • Manage Events and their coverage on social media
  • Enhance their communication skills (written, verbal and non-verbal)
  • Enhance and hone their photography skills
  • Understand Public Relations activities
  • Understand mass media of communication

Plan of Activities - Academic Year 2013-2014

  • Banner/Poster making competition (for all clubs of SSC, theme-Promoting their own clubs)
  • Sinhgad Karandak Banner Making Competition
  • Workshop on Photography
  • Session of writing skills by inhouse faculty

Apart form specific event the club would be offering following services

  • Monthly Newsletter of the SSC
  • Photography related help to any other event happening in Vadgaon Campus
  • Media and PR help for any event in the campus
  • Banners, brochures, invites, graphics etc
  • Social media propagations and news articles
